Thinking of You

For my dearest Mysu
The best day with you...
My Dearest Mysu, I was sitting here thinking about us today, and my mind kept drifting back to that day we spent at the mall in Saket. It’s funny how, of all the things we’ve done, that afternoon at the arcade stands out so clearly. I can still hear the chaos of the games, but mostly, I just remember your laugh over all of it.
I love this smile.
I keep picturing the way you looked when you were focused on winning—that competitive spark in your eyes that I love so much. In that moment, surrounded by everyone else in Delhi, it felt like there was a literal bubble around the two of us.
Being away from you right now is hard, but I close my eyes and I’m back in Saket, holding your hand and feeling like the luckiest person in the city. I built this little space for you so you’d have a place to come whenever the distance feels a bit too heavy.
Every day we are apart is just a countdown until the next time we’re making memories like that again. I’m counting the days until our next "arcade date."
Counting down the days...
Always yours,
Bubu
